Defending the Human Face

As centralized law enforcement databases and commercial big-tech scrapers run unchecked, automated algorithms non-consensually mine the behavior, image, and biometric likenesses of Black and Brown citizens. These extractions are processed inside hidden data-fusion modules to create flawed threat scores and synthetic digital replicas without public consent or compensation.

The Likeness Rights Vault is our decentralized counter-infrastructure. It functions as an environment built to help citizens anchor, track, and explicitly claim legal and cryptographic parameters over their digital human presence. By generating unique identity certificates, we establish an immutable record of ownership that disrupts unauthorized profiling.
