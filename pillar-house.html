Physical Community Blueprints

The Infrastructure Branch models spatial parameters, physical designs, and localized site layout criteria for our upcoming "Pillar House" facilities. By generating modular community architectures, we systematically scale our organizational footprint from Philadelphia across the nation. This branch focuses on shifting vulnerable urban areas into permanent zones of resilience, geometric accessibility, and secure physical data handling.
