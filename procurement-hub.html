Multi-Tier Subcontracting Framework

Solomon Kin LLC Technical Operations Partnership

To deliver enterprise-grade performance while remaining rooted to a strict non-profit mission, Thin Black and Brown Lines USA utilizes a sophisticated hybrid model. Under a compliant, formal Memorandum of Understanding (MOU), our organization retains Solomon Kin LLC to manage personnel, logistal coordination, and secure technology frameworks. This structured collaboration delivers significant procurement advantages to municipal review boards:

  • Strict Fiscal Insulation: All public funds flow directly to the primary non-profit entity, ensuring maximum compliance with municipal non-profit regulations and private-inurement parameters.
  • Enterprise-Grade Delivery: Personnel management, software portal operations, and spatial edge-computing deployments are executed via Solomon Kin LLC under the leadership of Chief Technology Officer and Principal Investigator Nehemiah M. Brown.
  • Scalable Deployment: Solomon Kin LLC retains the capacity to contract and deploy specialized independent field personnel (Tier 2 Subcontractors), insulating the prime contractor from operational liability while matching fluctuating program intake volumes.
