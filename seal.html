The Monumental Identity

The Great Seal
Registry Identifier: TBBLUSA.ORG-MMXX

The Official Great Seal of Black and Brown People of the United States of America stands as the definitive visual anchor and monumental identity of our organization. It represents the documentation of our collective existence, honoring Black and Brown citizens as the historic and modern pillars of American society.

In an era where identity is increasingly digitized, scraped, and processed by external software architectures, the Great Seal serves as a formal corporate shield. It represents our transition from defensive civil rights advocacy to proactive technological and structural sovereignty.

Legal & Structural Defenses

Copyright & Trademark Ownership

The Official Great Seal is fully copyrighted and proprietary under the legal control of TBBLUSA.ORG. This corporate ownership prevents predatory commercial enterprises from exploiting, repurposing, or diluting the definitive insignia representing the pillars of our community. Any unauthorized deployment or synthetic reproduction is subject to strict legal recourse.

Likeness Parameter Defense

We leverage our intellectual property frameworks to combat the unauthorized exploitation of minority cultural, artistic, and behavioral likenesses. By establishing a formalized legal registry, we challenge big-tech aggregators and data brokers that scrape identity elements for profit without providing equitable compensation or gaining human consent.

The Likeness Rights Vault

As centralized data-fusion networks compile invisible threat and behavioral profiles on individuals, the necessity for decentralized protection becomes vital. The Likeness Rights Vault is our technical initiative designed to counter algorithmic overreach. It functions as a secure structural framework where individuals can register, encrypt, and validate their digital likeness records.

Identity Protection

Utilizing cryptographic parameters to lock personal files and multimedia content before commercial big-tech scraping systems can ingest them.

Algorithmic Disruption

Providing toolsets to verify data tracking trails and issue formal requests to dismantle invisible, non-consensual threat profiling networks.

Sovereign On-Chain Verification
